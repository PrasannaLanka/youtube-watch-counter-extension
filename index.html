YouTube History Analyzer

How to get your YouTube Watch History file:
  1. Go to Google Takeout.
  2. Click Deselect all, then scroll down and check YouTube and YouTube Music.
  3. Click on All YouTube data included → select only History → click OK.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and click Next step.
  5. Choose .zip format and click Create export.
  6. Once ready, download and extract the .zip file.
  7. Find the file watch-history.html (or watch-history.json).
  8. Upload it below to see your stats!
